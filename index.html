About Us

Established in 2017, House of Handsome Norwich is a unique barbershop, situated in the beautiful Golden Triangle.

What makes us different? Our location is unique in that there are no long queues, unlike city barbers, and we have parking literally outside the front door.

The name House of Handsome is inspired by our very own resident French Bulldogs, Frank and Fred. We are a dog friendly barbers and the shop regularly has visits from our clients 4-legged friends.
